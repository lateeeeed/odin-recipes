Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a recipe to make the world's best lasagna.

Please enjoy both the making of and consuming of this lasagna, as it contains no illegal meats or vegetables whatsoever.

May contain milk.

The Lasagna ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • egg

How to make Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instruction.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to layer Lasagna

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
